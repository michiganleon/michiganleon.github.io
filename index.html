About Me

I am Xuefeng Hu, a PhD student at Computer Science Department @ University of South California. advised by Prof. Ram Nevatia.
My current research interests include Computer Vision, Multi Modality Learning and Automatic Story Comprehension.
I obtained my B.S. degrees at both University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and a M.S. degree at Columbia University in the city of New York . I worked as a graduated student researcher with Prof. Shih-Fu Chang and Dr. Svebor Karaman in the DVMM Lab during my time at Columbia.
Further info can be found in my Curriculum Vitae (Updated December 2017).
